About classes in the studio

Usually classes begin with a stay in silence and a greeting to calm the mind and prepare for practice.

During the sessions, time is given for exercises to reduce stress.

Yoga classes in the studio or in a group can help you establish contact with like-minded people.

Then the students perform a series of yoga poses (asanas), which can vary from simple and easy to more complex and intense. The teacher describes each exercise in detail and gives instructions on how to perform it, and also makes sure that the poses are correct.

Directions

There are many directions and styles of yoga, which differ in techniques, emphasis on certain aspects of practice, complexity and level of tension. Many of the most common yoga directions are presented in our studio.

Hatha Yoga

One of the most common styles based on physical asanas and pranayama.

Vinyasa Yoga

A dynamic and flowing style in which asanas are associated with breathing.

Ashtanga Yoga

It is known for his physical intensity and the use of a sequence of asanas.

Yin Yoga

The Yin yoga style is based on the principles of the Taoist system.

Who will yoga suit?

"Yoga is available to everyone who really wants it. Yoga is universal"

Yoga is useful both for people who want to improve their health and for people seeking spiritual development: yoga helps to find new opportunities for thinking, to think deeply about ways to create a more spiritually creative life.
